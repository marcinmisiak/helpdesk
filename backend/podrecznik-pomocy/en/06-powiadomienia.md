# Notifications

The Helpdesk system keeps you informed about important events in several ways — so you never miss anything important.

## Email Notifications

The system automatically sends emails in the following situations:

| Event | Recipient |
|-------|-----------|
| New ticket | Administrator, assigned worker |
| Reply to a ticket | Requester, worker |
| Assigned to you | Worker |
| Removed from assignees | Worker |
| Ticket closed | Requester |
| Password reset | The user who requested it |

The subject of each email contains the system name configured by the administrator in square brackets, e.g. `[IT Helpdesk] New ticket #42`. Changing the application name in Settings immediately affects the subjects of all subsequent messages.

## In-App Notifications

Counters and alerts are displayed in the top bar and sidebar:

- **Red badge** on the ticket icon — new tickets waiting
- **My count** — active tickets assigned to you

Counters refresh automatically every 15 seconds.

## Push Notifications (Browser)

If you want to receive notifications even when the app is in the background:

1. Click the **🔔 Push** button at the bottom of the sidebar
2. Accept the permission in the browser dialog
3. You will now receive system alerts

To disable push notifications, click the button again.

> **Note:** Push notifications require browser permission and only work on devices with Web Push support. If your browser blocks permissions, change the site settings in your browser options.

## Notification Sound

The system can play a sound when new tickets arrive. The **🔊 Sound** toggle at the bottom of the sidebar lets you enable or disable this feature. The setting is saved in the browser.

## Toast Notifications

Short messages appearing briefly in the corner of the screen inform you about:
- A new ticket in the system
- A new message on your ticket
- A ticket being assigned to you

Click **Go →** in the notification to immediately open the related ticket.
