# Getting Started with the Helpdesk System

Welcome to the ticket management system! This guide will help you use all the features of the application.

## Logging In

The login page provides the authentication methods configured by your administrator:

**Microsoft or Google login** — if the administrator has set up OAuth integration, a "Sign in with Microsoft" or "Sign in with Google" button will appear on the login page. Click it and confirm your identity in the provider's window. Your account must use the same email address as your system account.

**Email and password login** — click **Other login methods** (if OAuth buttons are visible), then enter your email address and password and click **Sign in**.

If you have forgotten your password, use the **Forgot my password** link on the login page. A password reset link will be sent to your email address.

## Navigation

After logging in, you will find the navigation menu (sidebar) on the left side of the screen. You can collapse and expand it by clicking the ≡ button in the top bar.

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **My tickets** | Your active and closed requests |
| **Deferred** | Tickets postponed for later |
| **Calendar** | A calendar view of deadlines |
| **Help** | This user guide |

## User Panel

At the top of the sidebar you will see your initials, full name, and role in the system. Below are counters:

- **Mine** — number of active tickets assigned to you
- **Deferred** — number of deferred tickets

## Dark and Light Mode

At the bottom of the sidebar you will find a button to switch between light and dark mode. Your preference is saved in the browser.

## Signing Out

Click the **Sign out** button at the bottom of the sidebar to securely end your session.
